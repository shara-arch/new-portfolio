About Me

My Name is Sharon Moegi, I am an upcoming Software Developer and aspiring Data Scientist with a passion for building things that are both functional and beautiful.

My journey into Data Science began with a love for Mathematics and the elegance of procedural reasoning. What draws me most to problem solving is the clarity it offers — the ability to know whether an answer is right, wrong, or could be refined into something better. That feedback loop makes every challenge genuinely satisfying to work through.

On the Development side, front end work is where I get to bring my creative side to life. I love crafting vibrant, visually engaging websites that are as enjoyable to look at as they are to use. But great design, to me, goes beyond aesthetics, it means building technology that everyone can access and benefit from. I'm deeply passionate about inclusive development, creating experiences that reach and empower marginalized communities, including people with disabilities. I believe the web should work for everyone, and I want my work to reflect that commitment.

I'm driven by curiosity, precision, and the belief that good technology should be as intuitive as it is powerful. I'm excited to keep growing, building, and contributing to projects that make a real and meaningful impact.
